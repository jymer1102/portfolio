The One And Only

Jackson Weimer, otherwise known as jymer1102, or just "J", was born and raised in Springfield, Missouri. He is the first one in his family to do lots of the things he does to this day. Such as: triathlon, aviation, development, gaming, and owning a coffee business.

Development

J grew up adjusting and exploring new technology and software. He came to love the idea of learning how things work the way they do, and making them do that. He developed multiple apps. The reason? Because he wanted to learn, and he wanted to make something personal/

Flight

J grew up loving planes. When he turned 14, he went on his first discovery flight, and he fell in love with aviation. He has been flying ever since.

Game/Stream

J, of course, grew up like most boys. He loved video games. Eventually, he started thinking, "What if I could stream this decent gameplay?" and "How can I share this with the world and make content some people might like?" And that grew from there.

Religion

Growing up, J was surrounded by Christian parents, friends, and mentors. Eventually, in the summer of 2023, he chose to give his life to Christ. J tries to incorporate his love and passion for Christ into his videos, streams, and work, the best he can.
